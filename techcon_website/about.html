History of TechCon

TechCon started in 2010 as a small meeting of friends discussing the challenges they met while creating web pages and has since grown into a major event.

Over the years, it has continued to grow to a major event that is held annually

Our Mission

To foster innovation, collaboration and growing knowledge in the tech community

Our Mission

TechCon's mission is to foster innovation, collaboration, and knowledge sharing within the technology community.

We strive to connect individuals, inspire new ideas, and drive positive change in the world through technology.
